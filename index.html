Cats Poem

Cats like to purr

Cats have fluffy fur

Cats always land on their feet

(apparently)

Cats enjoy getting a treat

Like tuna,a tasty fish

Served in only the best

porcelain dish

Cats love fresh milk and so do I

Cats are more amazing than

seeing a rainbow in the sky

Yes cats I do adore

I hope this little poem has not been a bore
